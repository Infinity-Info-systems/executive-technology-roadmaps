# Transformation Maturity Model

## Stage 1: Reactive

- work is ad hoc
- priorities change often
- outcomes are unclear

## Stage 2: Planned

- roadmaps exist
- ownership is emerging
- reviews are inconsistent

## Stage 3: Defined

- roadmap structure is consistent
- governance exists
- outcomes are more measurable

## Stage 4: Managed

- progress is tracked
- risks are reviewed regularly
- priorities are clearer

## Stage 5: Optimized

- transformation is measured and improved over time
- governance is embedded in execution
