# Executive Governance

## Purpose

Executive governance defines how roadmaps are reviewed, approved, funded, and adjusted.
It keeps the roadmap aligned to leadership priorities while making the decision path visible.

## Governance Practices

- establish a recurring executive review cadence
- keep decisions and actions visible
- assign owners to each initiative
- review risks and dependencies regularly
- track open decisions and blocked items explicitly
- separate status reporting from decision escalation

## Use

Use this page to explain how leaders keep the roadmap aligned to business priorities and delivery reality.

## Governance Questions

- which initiatives are on track?
- what is blocked?
- what needs executive decisions?
- what changed since the last review?
- where do we need a decision now?

## Outputs

- governance review notes
- action log
- decision record
- escalation list

## Operating Principle

Governance should reduce ambiguity, not create ceremony. The page should support fast review, clear ownership, and decisive follow-up.
It should also make it easy to see where the roadmap is healthy and where it needs intervention.
